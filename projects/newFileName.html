Freedom Project

Brandon Pinos

My freedom project took a lot of time and research, and a lot of planning into it. This project is about the facinating art and the technolgy used in construstion. I chose this as my freedom project because my dad is a construction worker, and I work with him when he needs my help. He shown the tools, and the technology being used in his workplace, and how they use the blueprints to make a drawing come to real life. Whaat I have made is a website listing technology used in present construction projects, and the future for construction, such as an invention I have in mind. There were many challenges faced in this project is adjusting the height and width on my images. The images that I have used were pretty big, and I have not made any changes to them. This will be something I will do to improve the quality of my Website in the future. Another struggle I faced was learning the positions on A-frame to make my invention. I gained a lot of knowledge from watching youtube videos and experimenting on my sandbox on my IDE, as well making a mini solar system to experiment the A-FRAME positons.
